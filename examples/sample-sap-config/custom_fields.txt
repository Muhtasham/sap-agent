Business Logic Notes
--------------------

1. ZZPRIORITY determines automatic discount application:
   - Priority 1: 15% discount
   - Priority 2: 10% discount
   - Priority 3: 5% discount
   - Priority 4-5: No automatic discount

2. ZZREFERRAL tracks lead source for marketing analytics

3. ZZREGION used for territory-based commission calculations

4. ZZPROMO (header and item level) can be combined:
   - Header promo applies to entire quote
   - Item promo can override for specific items

5. ZZAPPROVER triggers workflow:
   - If populated, quote requires approval before conversion
   - Entry created in ZQUOTE_APPROVAL table
   - Email notification sent to approver

6. ZZWARRANTY affects pricing:
   - Standard warranty: 12 months (no charge)
   - Extended warranty: 24/36 months (additional cost)

7. Custom Z tables enable:
   - Full audit trail of quote modifications
   - Multi-level approval workflow
   - Reporting and analytics
